Marketing Strategy

koothu | 4th July 2026

A phased marketing approach designed to build awareness, establish trust, foster community, and drive measurable conversions for Lifed.

Proposal Objectives

1

Build Brand Awareness

Become the first platform people think about when they think about holistic wellbeing.

2

Build Trust

Position Lifed as the most credible wellbeing platform through education, storytelling and expert voices.

3

Acquire Healthmates

Create a strong pipeline of practitioners, wellness centres and communities joining the platform.

4

Acquire Wellbeing Seekers

Generate consistent bookings through organic content, paid media and partnerships.

5

Build Community

Create a loyal audience that follows Lifed even before making a booking.

4
Phase 4

Conversion

Objective

Convert awareness, education, and community engagement into active users, Healthmates, corporate partnerships, and long-term platform engagement.

Core Message (Seeker)

"Your wellbeing journey begins here. Discover the right programme today and build a healthier tomorrow with personalised guidance from Lifed."

Conversion Goals

Wellbeing Seekers
  • Register on the Lifed App & Website
  • Complete the LEWIS Wellbeing Assessment
  • Receive a personalised Wellbeing Insight Report
  • Discover AI-powered programme recommendations
  • Book their first wellbeing programme
  • Follow a personalised wellbeing roadmap with suggested next-step programmes
  • Develop consistent wellbeing habits through Small Habits
  • Return for future programmes based on their wellbeing progress
  • Become active members of the Lifed ecosystem
Healthmates
  • Become a verified Healthmate
  • Register through the Healthmate Portal
  • Schedule a discovery call or product demo
  • Complete onboarding and verification
  • Co-create and publish curated programmes
  • Manage programmes through the Healthmate Dashboard
  • Grow bookings and community visibility
Corporate
  • Generate corporate wellbeing enquiries
  • Launch employee wellbeing programmes
  • Develop long-term corporate partnerships
  • Implement organisation-wide wellbeing initiatives
  • Measure employee wellbeing outcomes through the Lifed ecosystem

Customer Journey

Lifed's marketing is designed to guide users through a continuous wellbeing journey rather than a one-time programme booking. Every touchpoint encourages deeper engagement.

1

Users discover Lifed through digital marketing, PR, and real-world experiences.

  • Social
  • Search
  • YouTube
  • PR
  • Influencers
  • Events
2

Stage 2 – Understand

Users understand holistic wellbeing and Lifed's curated approach.

  • Web
  • Blog
  • Webinars
  • Podcasts
  • Founder Content
3

Stage 3 – Join

Users register and create their wellbeing profile to begin a personalised journey.

  • Lifed App

  • LEWIS Assessment
